Undo Last Action

Pressing this button reverts the most recent MediaMaid operation — rename, Smart Fix, or Nuke Downloads — restoring all files to their original locations and names.
How it works: MediaMaid keeps a rolling history of up to 10 operations. The button title shows the count of available undos. Each press reverts one batch.

Note: Undo cannot recover files that were deleted externally after a MediaMaid operation.
